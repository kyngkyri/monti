About Me

I attend a highschool in Harlem called Democracy Prep CHarter High School and after summer vacation is over, I will return to school as an official senior. As a scholar of Democracy Prep, I can honestly say that it is a great high school that I would recommend to parents because the school has a great cirriculum. Democracy Prep is a school that lets scholars know that when they're think they're done, they aren't, and that there are other challenges for them to stand up to. Democracy Prep offers many AP classes, which I have taken some in my junior year and will continue to take more during my senior year. During my junior year, I had an AP English teacher by the name of Brittany Long who made her class work on blog posts for a project, and I will forever love her for it because she gave scholars an opportunity to try something new and I ended up liking making blog posts. If you want to check out my blog posts, the link to my blog page is montisblogblog.wordpress.com.
